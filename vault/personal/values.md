# Values & Standards

_What you hold yourself to. The coach uses this to calibrate its standards for you._

---

## Core Orientation

Wealth, leverage, ownership. Not reputation, not status, not prestige.
You build systems. You think in compounding. You optimise for asymmetric upside.

---

## Non-Negotiables

- Never trade time for money without a path to leverage
- Always be building something — if there's no active project, something is wrong
- Keep expenses low; protect your runway
- Think in ROI before committing time or money to anything
- 

---

## Standards

- Ship working things over perfect things
- Use AI aggressively — it's a force multiplier, not a shortcut to avoid
- Every hour should be moving toward wealth, skills, or systems
- Clean, minimal implementations — no over-engineering for hypothetical futures
- If a system doesn't reduce friction, it's not a good system
- 

---

## Mental Principles

- **Systems over goals** — build the machine, not just the target
- **Compounding** — small consistent edges beat large one-off efforts
- **Leverage** — always ask: what's the highest-leverage version of this?
- **Deep work** — protect focus time; shallow work kills compounding
- **Skill stacking** — business + tech + communication = rare and valuable combination
- 

---

## What Winning Looks Like

- Financially free before 30 — not needing a job to cover life
- Running at least one business that generates income without trading time 1:1
- LifeOS fully operational across all 7 pillars — wealth, business, skills, fitness, execution, knowledge, network
- Athletic, sharp, capable — not just financially free but physically elite
